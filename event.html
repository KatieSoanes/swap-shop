Event Rules

  • Each attendee must bring a minimum number of 5 items to our Swap Shop event, you can bring a maximum of 15 items.
  • There is no entry fee, however, you just must fill out the sign up form to get a spot to swap in our event. At present our venue space holds 45 people so you must be early to guarantee yourself a spot to swap.
  • At the moment this event is for women’s clothing only (we hope to expand to men's soon).
  • Items must be in immaculate condition, if they are not new with tags they must be cleaned, and there can be no damages, stains, etc.
  • No one can bring swimwear or underwear as these are unhygienic to swap.
  • Upon arrival, you must check-in at our check-in desk, your items will be reviewed by one of our team members to make sure your items are in good condition for swapping.
  • Once you have checked in our host will bring you to your allocated rail space where you can set up your clothing and get prepared for our fabulous event.
  • Once you are set up we will have a tea, coffee, prosecco and snack area. You can get socialising with fellow swappers.
  • As long as you are happy any items not swapped will be donated to Enable Ireland.
